Welcome to UamuziBora Labs

Uamuzibora Labs is web based application to generate statistics about HIV patients in the Uamuzibora EMR system

Learn more

UamuziBora Labs provides the following types of statistics:

Clinic Statistics

 Statistics about the patient population. Ie. how many patients admitted per month

Distribution of Numerical Variables

 Shows the distribution of variables about patients. Ie the age distribution of patients.

Counting Statistics

 Counts the number of patients in different groups and sub groups. Ie how many patients of different genders.

Scatter Plots

 Scatter plots of variables about patients. Ie scatter plot of the height and weight of patients.

Compare Numerical Variables

 Compare Numerical variables for different groups of patients. Ie compare the mean age of females and males.
